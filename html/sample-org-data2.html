Service Profile: The Brighton Neighborhood Association (BNA) evolved from a grassroots movement to declare war against neighborhood deterioration. Since 1979, BNA has been instrumental in expediting the assimilation of multiple immigrant populations through advocacy and education. Current activities include tenant advocacy, crime prevention, encouragement of commercial revitalization, and advocacy for all segments of the Brighton community.
