Service Profile: The Albany County Rural Housing Alliance (ACRHA) is a HUD certified housing counseling agency offering education and individual assistance to anyone in need of housing or housing related services. The main office is located in Voorheesville and our satellite offices are located in Ravena and Cohoes.
The ACRHA service area that has been designated by the NYS Division of Housing & Community Renewal [DHCR] consists of the Towns of Berne, Knox, Westerlo, Rensselaerville, New Scotland, Coeymans, Green Island, the City of Cohoes and the Villages of Altamont, Voorheesville, and Ravena. ACRHA has ten housing related programs running concurrently to assist homeowners that fall below 80% of HUD median income with necessary home repairs: 1) ACRHA's Home Repair Program; 2) ACRHA's Revolving Loan Fund; 3) Coeymans Landing Heritage Fund; 4) RESTORE (emergency home repair program for senior homeowners); 5) HOME Program; 6) Emergency Home Repair Program; 7) Acquisition /Rehab Program; 8) New York Main Street Program for the Village of Ravena, 9) HUD Certified Housing Counseling Program and 10) the National Foreclosure Mitigation Counseling Program. The Albany County Rural Housing Alliance also helps homeowners complete and package USDA 504 Loan/Grants, a program for specific rural geographic locations as stipulated by USDA Rural Development. Loans of up to $20,000 at a 1% interest rate with a term of 20 years are available. These home repair loans are available to applicants who earn 50% of median income and are offered as grants if the homeowner is 62 years of age or older and cannot afford the loan. Grants are capped at a level of $7,500. A combination loan/grant may be made if the applicant is able to repay only part of the total cost of needed repairs. ACRHA is designated by the USDA to package USDA 502 mortgages as well. In addition, ACRHA manages 24 units of rental assisted senior housing in Feura Bush. ACRHA has and will continue to work with local municipalities and private corporations to apply for and administer New York State Affordable Housing Corporation grants primarily for project based down payment assistance programs. For further information and applications please visit our website at www.acrha.org.
